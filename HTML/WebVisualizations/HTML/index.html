Why?

[Limbic Brain!] [work in progress]

Everything I do, I believe in crafting/enabling holistic solutions, in understanding the context, the costs, the tradeoffs of any one effort, and in making them known. I believe that if I can figure out the problems of climate change and energy, I can start to cross the chasm gap between innovators, into the early majority.

  • I believe in thinking differently,
  • in honoring that great ideas are often born from terrible ones,
  • and that the context of any one person's life is important to hear.
  • I believe in breaking down the silos that keep knowledge separated.
  • I believe that the more I know, the more I can share with the world, and the more I can work to pay my reparations to the world for any devastations that my family has enabled or accompliced.
  • I believe that together, compassionately, we can make a difference.
  • I believe in the beauty and magic of science, and of the natural world.
